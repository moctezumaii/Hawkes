Comment: It would be helpful to the reader to provide a little more background information regarding the life-history and timing of natural origin smolts in this system somewhere in the introduction. Do these fish have a strict one-year residence in freshwater as juveniles before outmigrating to the ocean? Are there other juvenile life history strategies that buffer the population? Maybe there is some trapping data to relate the timing of your study fish to when fish would historically outmigrate?
